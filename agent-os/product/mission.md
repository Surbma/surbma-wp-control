# Product Mission

## Problem

WordPress site owners, administrators, and agencies lack a simple, zero-friction way to check the health and optimization status of a WordPress installation. They often need multiple plugins or manual investigation to understand what needs attention and to apply the right settings. This creates friction, especially for professionals running WordPress Optimization as a service.

## Target Users

- **WordPress site owners and administrators** who want to understand and optimize their own sites
- **WordPress agencies and freelancers** who manage multiple client sites and need quick status overviews
- **WordPress Optimization business clients** who rely on professional guidance to improve their installations

## Solution

Surbma | WP Control follows a **zero-step status check philosophy**: install the plugin and immediately see what needs attention — no configuration required. It acts as a lightweight, focused admin toolkit that checks the most important data of a WordPress installation and provides actionable controls to optimize it. Features are added incrementally, all serving the core purpose of status checking and settings management. It is the public companion plugin to the private WordPress Optimization business tooling.
